Our Story

Built by Linguists & NLP Experts, for Real Fluency

Founded in Salem, India — LingoFlow exists to eliminate language barriers and optimize conversational learning through cognitive AI.

Mission

Democratising Immersive Acquisition

LingoFlow's mission is to make high-performance, real-time conversational practice and learning tools accessible for every student — from self-directed language enthusiasts to schools and multinational corporations.

Our learning models link seamlessly, letting researchers in Salem, India collaborate and resolve translation problems, vocabulary acquisition curves, and grammar modules in lockstep with global educational institutions.

Vision 2030

To automate and customize 90% of vocabulary retention blocks through adaptive spaced repetition (SRS) and LLM-powered context models, freeing students to practice speaking.
